Who I Am

Hello! I’m Udani Amasha, a passionate and detail-oriented Computer Science undergraduate at the University of Westminster. I’m driven by curiosity about how technology shapes the way we live, connect, and evolve as a society. My academic journey has equipped me with a strong foundation in software development, algorithms, and data structures, while also inspiring me to think critically about the ethical and social dimensions of innovation.

Beyond the classroom, I explore the intersection of code, culture, and ethics through my personal blog, “Social & Tech.” I’m deeply committed to creating technology that is not only functional and innovative, but also accessible, ethical, and human-centered. Whether I’m writing code, reflecting on AI’s social impact, or learning something new, I strive to grow as a thoughtful developer and lifelong learner dedicated to positive digital change.
